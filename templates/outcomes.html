Analysis

Adoptions were the most common outcome for both feline and canine cases. Transfers and return-to-owner make up the remaining majority of outcomes for the shelter. It is somewhat surprising to note that return-to-owner outcomes are almost all canine cases. It is possible that these situations simply involved lost dogs that were brought to the shelter.
